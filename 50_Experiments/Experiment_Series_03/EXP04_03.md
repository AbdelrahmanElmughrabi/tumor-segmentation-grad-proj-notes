# EXP04_03: Combatting Overfitting

## Goal
Improve generalization using data augmentation and harder classification loss.

## What Was Done
* Added RandFlip and RandRotate (15°) to the training pipeline.
* Replaced BCE with Focal Loss ($\gamma=2.0, \alpha=0.25$).

## Outcomes
* ✅ Gradients now prioritize harder classification cases.

## In Plain Words
We gave the AI "vision exercises" by flipping and rotating the images so it doesn't just memorize the photos. We also used a new math formula to make it focus more on the tumors it was getting wrong.
