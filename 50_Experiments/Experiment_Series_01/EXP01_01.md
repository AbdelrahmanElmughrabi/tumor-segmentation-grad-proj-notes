# EXP01_01: Data Archaeology

## Goal
Clean raw CBIS-DDSM metadata into a binary-labeled, leakage-free master index.

## What Was Done
* Removed uncertain BI-RADS 3 cases.
* Mapped pathology to binary `Label` column.
* Assigned 70/10/20 patient-level splits.
* Verified spatial alignment of Full Mammograms and ROI masks.

## Outcomes
* ✅ Leakage-free split confirmed.
* ✅ Ready-to-train `CBIS_Master_Index_Clean.csv` generated.

## In Plain Words
We cleaned the messy raw data and made sure the AI doesn't "cheat" by seeing the same patient twice. We turned medical notes into simple 0s and 1s.
