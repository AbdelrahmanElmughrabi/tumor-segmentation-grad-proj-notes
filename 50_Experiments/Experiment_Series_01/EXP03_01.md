# EXP03_01: Skeleton MTL Architecture

## Goal
Assemble the MTL architecture and verify connectivity via dummy forward pass.

## What Was Done
* Defined shared EfficientNet-B0 encoder.
* Attached UNet++ decoder and CBAM classification head.
* Ran dummy tensor to confirm shape outputs.

## Outcomes
* ✅ Model has ~6.1M trainable parameters.
* ✅ Outputs correct mask (2, 1, 512, 512) and score (2, 1).

## In Plain Words
We built the "brain" and the "pen." We confirmed the plumbing works by feeding it a fake image before starting real training.
