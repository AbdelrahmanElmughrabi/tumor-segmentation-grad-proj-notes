# EXP05_02: The Mask Alignment Trap

## Goal
Evaluate performance of the mixed perspective strategy.

## What Was Done
* Full training run on mixed dataset.

## Outcomes
* ❌ Test Dice dropped: 0.228 → 0.137.
* ❌ Test AUC dropped: 0.783 → 0.751.
* **Key Finding:** Pairing zoomed-in crops with zoomed-out masks "poisons" the gradients.

## In Plain Words
It backfired. Because the "answer key" (the mask) was for the whole breast, it didn't line up with the zoomed-in photos. The AI got confused and performed worse.

## What It Feeds Into
* Decision in Experiment Series #3 to simplify and focus on Masses.
