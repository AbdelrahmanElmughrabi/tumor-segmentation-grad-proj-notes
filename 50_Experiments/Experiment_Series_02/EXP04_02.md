# EXP04_02: Mixed Training Logic

## Goal
Implement a training loop that processes both global context and local detail.

## What Was Done
* Created `MixedCBISDDSMDataset` to yield two passes per row: FULL and CROP.
* Both passes linked to the same ROI mask.

## Outcomes
* ✅ Pipeline successfully doubles training data on-the-fly.

## In Plain Words
We told the AI: "Look at the whole breast, then look at the zoomed-in tumor." We wanted it to learn both the location and the texture.
