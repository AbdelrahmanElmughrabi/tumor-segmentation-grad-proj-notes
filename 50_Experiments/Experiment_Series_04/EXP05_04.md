# EXP05_04: Staged Training Results

## Goal
Push Dice above 0.75 and recover classification AUC.

## What Was Done
* Full staged training run.

## Outcomes
* ✅ AUC recovered: 0.780.
* ✅ False Positives halved: 56 → 28.
* ❌ False Negatives increased: 29 → 51.
* ⚠️ Dice plateaued at 0.524.

## In Plain Words
The AI is much more careful now and makes fewer "false alarm" mistakes. However, it is missing some real tumors. We've hit a limit because the tumors are just too small in the big breast images.
