# EXP04_04: Staged Training Strategy

## Goal
Prevent classification noise from interfering with spatial learning.

## What Was Done
* Implemented Phase 1: Freeze classification, train segmentation only (10 epochs).
* Implemented Phase 2: Unfreeze and train both tasks.
* Replaced Dice with Tversky Loss ($\alpha=0.3, \beta=0.7$).

## Outcomes
* ✅ Segmentation gradients established a solid spatial foundation first.

## In Plain Words
We taught the AI in two steps. First, we just taught it how to find the tumor without worrying about what type it was. Once it was good at finding it, we turned on the classification part.
